Fitness Node
In the heart of Nad Al Sheba, you'll find the Fitness Node, a vibrant celebration of physical wellbeing and community togetherness. It boasts a wide range of facilities, including recreational swimming pools, racquet sports, basketball courts, and skate parks, all seamlessly connected to a picturesque 3-kilometre jogging track and cycling routes.

This hub not only promotes physical health but also nurtures social connections, with ample open spaces for community events and opportunities for spectators.

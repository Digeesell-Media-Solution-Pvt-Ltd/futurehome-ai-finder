ABOUT Dubai Maritime City

Surroundings That Echo the Quiet Within

Prestige One is shaping one of the most defining landmarks in Dubai Maritime City — our largest and most ambitious waterfront development to date. Designed with precision, elevated hospitality, and a vision that matches the district’s future, this project stands as a statement of progress and a new benchmark for luxury coastal living in Dubai.

Rooftop infinity pool with skyline views, waterfront living, and resort-style amenities crafted for sunset moments.
Outdoor Cinema – A premium theatre experience is just around the corner, with plush seating and curated film selection.

Schools

Jumeira Baccalaureate School

International Baccalaureate school on Al Wasl Rd (Jumeirah 1), a short drive from DMC; strong continuum from PYP to DP.

St. Mary’s Catholic High School (UK curriculum, Oud Metha)

Established in 1968; long-standing British curriculum school in Oud Metha/Bur Dubai, within easy reach of DMC.

The Indian High School (CBSE, Oud Metha)

Flagship CBSE campus beside Oud Metha Metro; popular for K–12 with long track record in Dubai.

Dubai Gem Private School

UK National Curriculum from FS to Sixth Form (IGCSEs and A-Levels). Well-established campus in Oud Metha, about a 10–15-minute drive from Dubai Maritime City via Al Mina/Sheikh Rashid Rd.

Hospitals

Rashid Hospital (Oud Metha)

Major DHA tertiary/trauma hospital serving Dubai; one of the city’s most established facilities, a short drive from DMC.

Aster Hospital, Mankhool (Bur Dubai)

24-hour multispecialty hospital near Sharaf DG, Kuwait Rd, Al Mankhool; convenient access from DMC via Port Rashid.

Iranian Hospital (Jumeirah 1)

General hospital on Al Wasl Rd (Jumeirah 1), close to DMC and the beachfront.

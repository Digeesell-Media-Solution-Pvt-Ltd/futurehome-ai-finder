Sea Cliff by Imtiaz

Wind & Tide

Seacliff by Imtiaz invites you to experience life guided by the rhythm of the ocean.

Nestled between shaded island pathways and sweeping open‑water views, it is a place where the skyline gently recedes, and the sea stretches into an endless horizon. Soft tones of light, sand, and air come together to reflect the warmth of the sun and the calming pulse of the tides, creating a refined sanctuary by the sea.

Ideally positioned at the forefront of Dubai Islands, Seacliff enjoys a prime waterfront address within a vibrant island community. Seamlessly connected to the mainland by established bridges and road networks, this retreat places the serenity of island living within easy reach of the city. Dubai International Airport, Downtown Dubai, and Dubai Creek are all conveniently accessible, offering a rare balance between tranquil seaside living and urban connectivity.

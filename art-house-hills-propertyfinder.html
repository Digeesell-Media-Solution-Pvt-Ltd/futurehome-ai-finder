      Arthouse Hills at Arjan is a refined residential destination where art, wellness, and contemporary living come together seamlessly. Inspired by the creative legacy of New York’s iconic Arthouse, the project offers an intimate collection of thoughtfully designed studio, 1- and 2-bedroom residences, each crafted to elevate everyday life. From striking architecture and curated interiors to spaces that feel both expressive and serene, every detail reflects a lifestyle that is personal, inspired, and effortlessly sophisticated.

      Set within the vibrant community of Arjan, Arthouse Hills invites residents into a world centered on well-being and connection. Wellness-focused amenities, tranquil green spaces, and social areas are designed to nurture both body and mind, creating a calm retreat from the pace of the city while staying closely connected to it. More than just a home, Arthouse Hills is a living experience—where creativity thrives, comfort is intentional, and every day feels thoughtfully designed for modern urban living.

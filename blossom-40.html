Blossom 40 – Thoughtfully Planned Living in Majan, Dubai

Blossom 40 reflects Tranquil Developers’ belief that homes should be built with thought. Located in Majan and shaped by its green surroundings, this boutique development is planned to support everyday living with clarity, comfort, and long-term relevance.

The project offers well-considered 1.5 bedroom and 2.5 bedroom apartments designed around how people live, work, and unwind. Layouts prioritise usable space, natural light, and a sense of ease, making the homes suitable for families, working professionals, and those choosing Majan as a long-term address.

Set within Majan Dubai Land, Blossom 40 benefits from established infrastructure, improved connectivity, and steady residential demand. The location supports both lifestyle stability and investment confidence, positioning the project as a considered choice among real estate developments in Majan.
